About Anchhi

Anchhi is an upcoming luxury fashion house based in South Delhi, created for individuals who believe style is more than clothing—it is expression, identity, and confidence.

We specialize in premium fashion wear, designer couture, and fancy dress collections that blend timeless sophistication with contemporary trends.

From elegant gowns and luxury ethnic wear to bold modern silhouettes, every piece is crafted with precision, artistry, and refined aesthetics.

At Anchhi, fashion is not just worn. It is experienced.

Meet The Master

Behind Anchhi stands the creative vision of Mr. Mir Irfan Mastar, the master designer shaping the identity of our brand.

Known for his sharp eye for detail and elegant design sense, Mr. Mastar brings years of fashion understanding, creativity, and artistic precision into every design.

His expertise lies in transforming fabrics into statement pieces that feel luxurious, modern, and timeless. Whether it is couture wear, fancy dresses, ethnic styling, or custom pieces, his designs are crafted to make every individual stand out with confidence.

Every Anchhi creation carries his signature touch—refined, bold, and unforgettable.
